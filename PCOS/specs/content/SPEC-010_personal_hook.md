# SPEC-010: The Personal Hook

## Rule ID
RULE-010

## Source
Rule_v8, Section III (Content Philosophy: E-E-A-T & Originality), Rule 10

## Lock Status
**[MUTABLE]** — Can be overridden by the Founder's explicit session-specific instruction.

## Requirement
Every article must start with a real-life workplace question or situation to establish first-hand experience. The hook must be:
- **Real** — an actual event, question, or situation from the Founder's workplace
- **Confirmed** — explicitly stated by the Founder, not invented by the AI
- **Opening** — placed at the start of the article, not buried later

## Mechanical Check
- **Validator:** None (qualitative — depends on Founder confirmation)
- **Enforcement:** Prompt instruction in PROMPT_research_gathering (SPEC-033 covers the gathering process)
- **Gate:** The Founder must confirm the hook before outlining begins

## Failure Consequence
**Block outline.** The article cannot proceed to H2 Outline (SPEC-036) without a confirmed personal hook.

## Dependencies
- SPEC-033 (Personal Hook Gather) — the process for collecting the hook
- SPEC-005 (No Fabricated Reactions) — the hook cannot contain invented reactions

## Override Rules
Can be overridden per-article by the Founder's explicit instruction. Override is one-time only.
